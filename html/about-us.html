About Us

The Public Utility Commission (PUC) Center is dedicated to ensuring fair and efficient regulation of public utilities. Our mission is to safeguard the interests of consumers, promote competition, and oversee the responsible delivery of essential services.

At the PUC Center, we are committed to:

  • Protecting consumer rights
  • Fostering a competitive and innovative marketplace
  • Ensuring the reliability and affordability of utility services

Our team of experts works tirelessly to analyze and regulate the utilities industry, making informed decisions that benefit both consumers and service providers.

Community Outreach

We take pride in our community outreach efforts. Over the years, we have conducted numerous PUC camps in the Pune and PCMC regions to promote awareness about vehicle emissions and pollution control.

Key Statistics:

  • Number of PUC Camps Conducted: 50+
  • Participants Reached: 1000+
  • PUC certificates issued per day :100+

Our commitment to environmental sustainability drives us to engage with the community and address concerns related to air quality and pollution control.

Our Values

Our work is guided by a set of core values:

  • Integrity
  • Transparency
  • Accountability
  • Public Service

We believe in open communication, fairness, and a commitment to public welfare. By upholding these values, we strive to build trust and confidence in the regulatory processes we oversee.
